That's the whole thing. Everybody wants the clever tool. The tools that actually get used are the boring ones that don't break.

Had a professor who spent a semester building an elaborate citation management system. Color-coded. Cross-referenced. Beautiful architecture. He still writes his citations on index cards because the cards don't crash.
